What is this game ?

DLS (Dream League Soccer) is a popular football game available on mobile devices, including those using Play Market. Developed by First Touch Games, it offers an exciting football experience with elements of management and strategy.
  • Main features of Dream League Soccer (DLS): Team creation and management:
  • In DLS, you can create your own football team, select players, improve the stadium, customize tactics and strategies. Realistic matches:
  • Players manage a team in real matches, with the ability to control each football player on the field, score goals and win tournaments. Game modes:
  • There are several game modes, including a career, where you lead your team to the heights of football Olympus, and multiplayer, where you can compete with players from all over the world. Updates and real players:
  • The game is regularly updated, including current squads and leagues. The game features real football players licensed by FIFPro. Team Customization:
  • In DLS, you can customize your team by changing the uniform, logos, and even the stadium. Buying and developing players:
  • You can buy new players, train them, and improve their characteristics to improve their performance on the field. Why the game is popular: Availability: Dream League Soccer is available for free on the Play Market, with in-game purchases. Graphics and controls: The game offers good graphics and convenient controls, which makes it attractive to fans of football simulators.
  • Community: The ability to compete with other players around the world attracts a large number of players.
DLS is one of the best football simulators on mobile platforms, providing rich functionality and exciting gameplay.
